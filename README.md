## What AS9100 Certification Means

AS9100 is a globally recognized aerospace quality management standard developed by the **International Aerospace Quality Group (IAQG)**. It expands on ISO 9001, adding specialized requirements for aerospace, defense, and aviation applications.  

AS9100 ensures that organizations maintain:
- Total traceability of products and materials  
- Rigorous documentation and risk management  
- Preventive and corrective action systems  
- Continuous improvement and customer satisfaction focus  
- Strict control over counterfeit prevention and supplier quality  

Masline’s AS9100 certification affirms its ability to deliver components that meet exacting aerospace and defense specifications while maintaining full compliance with international quality and regulatory requirements.
